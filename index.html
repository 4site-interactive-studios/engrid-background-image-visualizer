Background Image Helper

What this preview shows

The canvas shows your image (or its current crop). The sidebar represents the form column on the page — its width matches Form width and its position matches Form position.

  • Image safe zone

    The vertical column corresponding to your focal point. If your focal point lands here, the image will hold up across viewports.

  • Image warm zone

    Five 30px bands on either side of the safe zone, stepping down by 20% opacity per band. It is clipped at the image edge for left and right focal points.

  • Form area

    The sidebar itself. Its width equals Form width, anchored per Form position. Anything behind the sidebar would be hidden by the form on the real page.
