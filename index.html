Background Image Helper

What this preview shows

The canvas shows your image (or its current crop) with overlays that preview how it will hold up against your form layout settings.

  • Image safe zone

    A vertical column at the position of your focal point, sized to match Image safe zone. If important content lands here it will hold up across viewports.

  • Image warm zone

    Five 30px bands on either side of the safe zone, stepping down by 20% opacity per band. Clipped at the image edge for left and right focal points.

  • Focal section indicator

    A dashed white circle horizontally centered on the safe zone column at the row matching your focal point. Its diameter is 75% of the safe zone width, shrunk if the crop is too short to maintain matching top/bottom clearance.

  • Center form position

    When Form position is set to Center, the safe zone column represents the form itself: shown in red on the main preview, and as solid black on the crop preview (since the form covers it).

  • Keyboard nudging

    Click the preview to focus it, then use the arrow keys to nudge the crop one preview-pixel at a time. Hold Shift to nudge by 10 pixels.
